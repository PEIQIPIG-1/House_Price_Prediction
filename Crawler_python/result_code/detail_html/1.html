Property details

Listing # 855573 | Listed on 11 October

Step inside and be captivated by the exquisite craftsmanship and attention to detail that defines every aspect of this residence. This prestigious freehold development 'Proxima Residences' presents an exceptional opportunity to own a stunning 3+ bedroom, 2-bathroom penthouse level apartment, boasting a spacious 212m2 including the 28m2 private balcony. The open and airy layout creates a seamless flow between the living, dining, and kitchen areas, making it ideal for both entertaining guests, and enjoying quality time with family. The addition of a media room in this luxury residence is sure to get the tick from the family.

The well-appointed bedrooms provide ample space for relaxation, while the two luxurious bathrooms offer a touch of indulgence and convenience, especially since the ensuite has a bathtub to relax all your worries away. Wake up to breathtaking views of urban Auckland from the top floor and embrace all that surrounds you.

With best-in-class fit out and a design focused on space, you can look forward to entertaining friends and family in your designer kitchen equipped with European/Miele appliances. Sleek contemporary aesthetics of large cabinetry & beautifully engineered stone benchtop.

Construction is underway by Award Winning Kalmar - proudly NZ owned with estimated completion due for 2nd quarter 2024. Proxima Residences is a vision built by industry leaders aiming to be the most exclusive address in Eden Terrace.

You have the exclusive opportunity to purchase separate carpark spaces. Choose between a single carpark equipped with electric car charging capabilities for $100k or a tandem carpark for $170k, ensuring both convenience and sustainability.

Located near Ponsonby, Mt Eden and Kingsland, Eden Terrace is a vibrant upcoming area. Proxima is in a prime position with easy access to your favourite coffee shops, boutique shopping, parks, and some of the best restaurants in Auckland like the French Cafe and Kazuya. Handy to motorways & the link bus route. Only a few minutes walk to K Road which has been ranked one of the top 6 coolest streets in the world according to TimeOut Magazine, not to mention the brand new train station nearby allowing you to benefit & capitalize most from that $5+ billion dollar investment.

Don't miss this exceptional opportunity to secure your dream home! Please contact me to arrange a time to have a look through the sales suite.
